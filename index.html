ABOUT US

When you work with us, you are part of a team of committed collaborators. All aspect of our software development are intergrated, interactive and agile.

Our approach unifies design, development and product management to create exceptional products.

We start each project by understanding your business goals, the user's and the product requirements.

Then we help translate them into effective and elegant solutions.

SERVICES

Before we sign a contract or write a line of code our team will spend the necessary time needed to understand your product vision.

The outcome of this scoping session is a proposal or high-level statement of work.

Based on your requirements, we can provide a complete offering or just the pieces you need.

WHAT WE DO


DESIGN

Our design practise offers a full range of services including brand strategy, interaction, visual design and user experience through testing. Throughout your project, our designers create and implement visual design and work flows, solicit user feedback and work with you to make sure what gets built is what is needed.


DEVELOPMENT

All engineers are fluent in the latest enterprise, mobile and web developement technologies. They collaborate with your team to write adn improve on a daily basis, using proven practises such as test-driven development and pair-programming.


PROCUCT MANAGEMENT

Planning and Development is interactive, because we are constantly coding and testing, the products we build are always ready to go live. This interactive process allows for changes as business requiremnts evolve.
